SERVICES

WEB DESIGN

Before we start to build your site we work on its design and create a graphical version of what it is going to look like. One of our web designers will take the elements of your brand and create a visual of your site so that you can approve the look and feel of the pages

If you are just getting started with your business and do not have a logo yet, or if you are thinking of a rebrand, we can help you with this step as well.

Once you approve the design of the website we will start building the interactive elements or HTML pages for the web. The two-dimensional design will become a fully functional and interlinked website.

WEB DEVELOPMENT

Depending on your particular business and your preferences your site may include various components like drop-down menus, enquiry and contact forms, Auto-Responders, Animations, Video and/or Social Media integration and thereof.

ECOMMERCE

eCommerce websites require a database structure behind them to manage the vast amounts of data such as product information, images and transaction history; those sites also require secure payment facilities so your customers are able to purchase products online.

GRAPHIC DESIGN & PRINT

Whether you need a logo for your new business, help with brand development or somebody to design your corporate livery – namely business cards, letterheads, presentation folders, flyers, brochures etc. – our graphic designers are here to help.

ONLINE MARKETING

Having a website is only one piece of the puzzle. You also need to market the website and make it as easy as possible for prospective clients to find you and get in touch with you.

WEBSITE MAINTENANCE

Did you know that an out-of-date site ranks lower on Google? Even worse, potential clients will be unable to see what current offers and services you are providing which means you might miss out on valuable leads.
